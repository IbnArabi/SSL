TEAMS HOME FIXTURES

Points & Rules
  1. Matches are of 15 overs
  2. 6 Balls per overs
  3. 5 Players per team
  4. Umpires decision is not able to be challanged
  5. One review per side, per inning
  6. Super over is only going to be played in the Qualifying rounds

Points Distrbution
  1. 2 Points given to winning side
  2. 1 Point given to each side in case of a tie
  3. 0 Points given to losing side
  4. Highest point, goes to top of table
  5. In case of points tie, position will be arranged regarding Net Run Rate (NRR)
  6. Top 4 teams qulaify for playoffs
